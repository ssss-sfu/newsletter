SSSS Committees

Have you ever wanted to help out with the SSSS but might be worried about the time commitment? Or maybe you're really passionate about merch, web development, or running events? Well in that case we have SSSS committees!
What are committees? They're an extension of the SSSS that focuses on one specific task, and membership is voluntary and automatic so you can help out your student society in a way that works for you.

Here are the 3 committees we currently have in place:


Website

After finishing some fantastic work on the SSSS website you can see here: www.sfussss.org, the Website committee is off to expand to even greater web dev projects! Join the team to check out what cool stuff they're cooking up and how you can contribute. All skills levels are welcome.

Merch

Designing merch for the SSSS, what kind of merch? Whatever you think would be cool! Last year the merch team designed buttons, stickers, lanyards, and t-shirts for Frosh, as well as the RootHacks t-shirts and sticker page.

Events

The SSSS runs a lot of events throughout the year, and you can help pitch, organize, and run some of these events in our Events Committee! Whether you just want to volunteer your time the day of, or help create an event from the ground up, this is the place for you.

If you're interested, head to the SSSS discord channel and check out #what-are-committees channel to get started!
